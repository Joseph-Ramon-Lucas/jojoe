Leave The World Behind 10 Very suspenseful film that kept expanding with mystery and unanswered questions. At first, the problem is on the two people who visit the house, but then it shifts to more than that. The overwhelming apocalyptic undertone shifts the movie away from a simple home intrusion movie which is an amazing way to keep the plot interesting and always moving. The ending felt confusing with lots of different events occurring yet not feeling like they've concluded. Overall, it's done very well Joe
Big Eyes 7 Very predictable and quite corny at times with the exaggerated actions that the husband got into. The whole premise of starting from nothing and lying your way until you become unfathomably successful is an interesting concept but can get very stale. It was interesting to see how far it would go but after a while I just wanted the movie to end. Joe
The Firm 7 Very serious movie where a young law graduate starts his career at a firm but learns how sketchy they are. It was interesting to see how the characters became more familiar with the people they worked with. Although slow at first, the intensity ramps up as our main character does some nefarious acts and tries to escape from the job he originally praised. Most of the characters were a bit creepy/exaggerated but the resolution to fix the main problem was a clever idea. Sometimes a bit hard to follow since the plot got very complicated. Joe
