Pho street Beef Ribeye Pho & Brisket 9.5 Decently priced soups with large portion sizes. The broths used are always full of rich meaty and bone flavor combined with the tangy citrus tastes that complement the rest of the soup ingredients. Just wish the meat were more tender sometimes. Joe
El Taco Chicken Tacos with Cilantro Creamy sauce & a fajita bowl. 7.5 The tacos are alright. They are tasty, but not very authentic. Madi's Coffee Hot Lattes, Chai Lattes, and breakfast sandwiches. 7.5 Good enough to return and keep shopping for the ambience, but not good enough to love and rejoice right after you have their products. Sometimes the customers flirt with the baristas and sometimes the baristas flirt back- this makes the atmosphere more interesting to listen to. Lattes are nothing special, they're just alright. Perhaps a bit too sugary. Johanna
